Reed Piernock

Reed is a senior front-end web developer in Washington DC, specializing mobile first, responsive design using modern CSS techniques, mostly written with Dart Sass. These days, they are primary coding themes and pattern libraries for WordPress, with a bit of custom Gutenberg block work on the side. They are keenly interested in clean code, especially as it relates to usability and accessibility.

Reed also currently studies Communication, Culture, and Technology at the Graduate School of Arts and Sciences of Georgetown University, focusing on the representation of marginalized identities in popular media, specifically depictions of fat and gender. Their other interests include race and disability studies, the effects of trauma and abuse, and issues of addressing online harassment and hate speech.

When not working or researching, Reed is watching horror movies or anthologies, singing in a semi-competitive karaoke league, baking vegan treats, or knitting socks based on their fandoms.
